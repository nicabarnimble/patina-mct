## Decisions

- MCT is the whole Mother/Child/Toy product; comparative camelCase tokens are
  temporary namespace qualifiers, not code or product renames.
- Slate remains Slate and a future belief-centered Patina remains Patina; each
  may operate as an `mctChild`.
- `patinaMother` is a source of operational requirements and prior art.
  Accepted responsibilities are translated or rebuilt under MCT law rather
  than inherited wholesale.
- **Port** means intentional code reuse and requires justification;
  **replace** means `mctMother` takes over an operational responsibility.
- `patinaToy` and `mctToy` are not equivalent authority models.
- Living evidence links use pinned GitHub revisions; historical sessions and
  captured request records remain unchanged.
