#### Key decisions

- Lifecycle control remains owner-authenticated UDS fact ingress and never constructs an `MctCall`.
- launchd is an effect adapter; Mother owns lifecycle policy, evidence ordering, reconciliation, and readiness.
- Supervision is strict `gui/<uid>` only for this slice, with no user/system/Homebrew/detached fallback. Executable byte changes require explicit `install --replace`.
- Known writer loss removes readiness and blocks new protected effects. An in-progress effect may finish physically, but its outcome is neither acknowledged nor cached without durable terminal facts.
- Uninstall preserves ledger, identity, SQLite state, children/artifacts, blobs, and logs; a foreign plist is observed and preserved rather than deleted.
- [[evidence-claims-require-landed-proof]] captures that evidence claims require assertions and source citations. Passing code is not reworked merely because close-out prose overclaimed; proof is strengthened independently.
- Track 3 remained 19 `COVERED` and 6 `DEFERRED` for operational invariants because the continuation increased proof depth without changing invariant disposition.
